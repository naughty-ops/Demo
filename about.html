Our Journey

A timeline of milestones and achievements that shaped our institution

1994

Inception

AKNM Government Polytechnic College was established with a vision to provide quality technical education to students in the Malabar region of Kerala.

1996

First Batch Graduation

Our first batch of students graduated, marking the beginning of a legacy of producing skilled technical professionals.

2002

Infrastructure Expansion

Major infrastructure development including new laboratories, workshop facilities, and library expansion.

2010

AICTE Recognition

Received permanent affiliation from AICTE and introduced new diploma programs in emerging technical fields.

2018

Smart Campus Initiative

Implemented digital classrooms, high-speed campus WiFi, and e-learning platforms for enhanced education delivery.

2023

Current Excellence

Recognized as one of the top polytechnic colleges in Kerala with state-of-the-art facilities and industry collaborations.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our educational philosophy and institutional growth

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education that produces competent professionals with strong ethical values, capable of meeting global challenges and contributing to societal development through innovation and leadership.

Mission

To provide quality technical education through innovative teaching methods, industry collaboration, and research orientation. To develop skilled professionals with entrepreneurial mindset and social responsibility.

Quality Policy

We are committed to continuous improvement in all academic and administrative processes to achieve excellence in technical education and produce diploma holders who meet industry requirements and societal expectations.
